# Limitations

## Model size
0.5B models may not exhibit the same circuit structures as larger models. Findings may not transfer.

## Synthetic tasks
Toy tasks may not capture the full complexity of natural language behaviours.

## Architecture specificity
Circuit structures are architecture-dependent. Qwen-specific findings may not apply to other architectures.

## Training scale
Small-scale training runs may not reproduce effects seen at full training scale.

## Interpretability methods
Each method (ablation, patching, steering, probes, SAEs) has known failure modes. No single method is sufficient.

## Computational constraints
Limited to consumer hardware (8GB VRAM). Some experiments may require compromises.
